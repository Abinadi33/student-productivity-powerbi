## Project Objective
Validate the business hypothesis regarding whether student infrastructure (specifically internet quality) correlates with academic performance (GPA), sleep duration, and study habits.

## Methodology & Tools
- **Data Cleaning & ETL:** Power Query
- **Data Modeling & Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- **Data Analysis & Visualization:** Power BI (Exploratory Data Analysis & Trend Analysis)

## Key Findings & Operational Value
Through data segmentation and descriptive analysis, the dashboard validated that there is no operational correlation between internet quality and student performance. 

**Business Impact:** This insight allows stakeholders to redirect budgets away from infrastructure variables and focus on internal platform engagement and content quality to optimize the learning funnel.
